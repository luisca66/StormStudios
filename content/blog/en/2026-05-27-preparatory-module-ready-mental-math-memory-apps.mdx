Today Storm Studios Learning crosses a small but important threshold: the [preparatory module of the Harmony Course](/en/harmony-course) is now ready.

That means the entrance to the course is no longer a promise or a placeholder. There is now a concrete path for beginning the work: note writing, rhythm, intervals, and the sequencer. These are the minimum foundations that allow harmony study to begin on real musical ground instead of floating in abstraction.

Two apps that belong to the same training system are also ready:

- [Little Elephant Math](/en/apps/matematicas-mentales), a mental math app with 20 progressive levels, guided tutors, and timed play.
- [Memory App – Mnemonics](/en/apps/memoria), a tool for training memory through visual associations and active practice.

These apps are not decorative extras. The idea is that musical study needs more than written theory: it needs attention, working memory, processing speed, ear training, reading, and consistency. The preparatory module gives the musical foundation; the apps help train the cognitive abilities that support that foundation.

The goal remains the same: to build a free, serious, practical path for anyone who wants to study music deeply. No rush, but with direction.

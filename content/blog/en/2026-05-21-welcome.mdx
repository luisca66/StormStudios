I don't know how you found your way here. Maybe through a search, maybe through a link, maybe by pure chance. It doesn't matter. What matters is that you're here, and everything you find on this site is yours.

Storm Studios Learning is not a course platform. There are no plans, no tiers, no catch. It's a legacy: the knowledge I received from my teacher Humberto Hernández Medrano, who trained with Shostakovich at the Moscow Conservatory. That chain reaches all the way here, to this screen, to you.

What you'll find is not a transcript of what I learned. It's twenty-five years of professional practice distilled into a method that preserves the rigor of its origins and adds everything that real experience teaches — the kind no conservatory can give you.

You can start today with the propaedeutic course, which is already complete. The remaining lessons and the Android and iOS apps will arrive as I finish them. I build this site on my own, with the help of artificial intelligence and a great deal of care, thinking about the legacy I received and the one I want to leave behind.

If this is what you were looking for, welcome.

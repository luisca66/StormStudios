## Training with Storm Studios: Choose Your Path

At Storm Studios Learning, we are dedicated to guiding you in your transformation toward becoming a true **"Musical Being"**. We understand that each student has different needs and circumstances, so we offer various training modalities, all rooted in our integral philosophy with a special emphasis on deep **auditory training** as the fundamental pillar.

## The In-Person Workshop in Mexico City

Our most immersive and comprehensive format takes place in our **studio-gym in Mexico City**. It is designed for students who want concentrated work on traditional harmony, ear training, instrumental awareness and the broader Storm methodology.

- Traditional harmony, analysis and voice leading
- Ear training linked to real musical work
- Instrumental awareness, body use and disciplined practice

## Guided Study for Students Working at a Distance

Not every student can attend the in-person workshop. For that reason, the free course, the apps, the blog and the study guides also form a practical digital path. They let you begin serious work in harmony and listening wherever you are.

If you are studying remotely and want to identify the best route through the material, contact us. We can point you toward the most relevant starting point based on your current level and goals.

## Who This Is For

- Students who want a structured traditional harmony course
- Musicians who need stronger ear training and interval work
- Learners who prefer a serious method over fragmented tips
- Artists looking for a connection between theory, listening and practice

## Start with the Right Entry Point

Many students begin with the **Harmony Course**, continue with the **apps** for focused practice, and use the **blog** and **study guides** to deepen specific topics. Others are ready to discuss the in-person route immediately.

Ready to begin? [Contact us](/en/contact) and we'll help you find the right path.

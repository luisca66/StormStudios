## Welcome to the Traditional Harmony Course

This course is the direct transmission of the method that master **Humberto Hernández Medrano** taught for decades at the National Conservatory of Music in Mexico — a method rooted in the tradition of **Dmitri Shostakovich** and the finest Western music pedagogy.

Today that legacy comes to you, completely free, online.

---

## What will you learn?

By the end of this course you will be able to:

- Write 4-voice chorales (SATB) following classical rules
- Recognize and apply fundamental harmonic progressions from the tonal repertoire
- Use inversions, cadences, and modulations with precision
- Understand *why* chords sound good (or bad) — not just memorize it
- Correct your own exercises with the help of the **Virtual Teacher**

---

## Why study classical harmony in the age of AI?

We live in a paradoxical moment: AI can generate music in seconds, but musicians who understand *why* harmony works are more valuable than ever.

Knowing harmony allows you to:

- **Guide** AI with judgment — not just accept what it generates
- **Compose** with intention, not just by accident
- **Analyze** any music you hear
- **Teach** with solid foundations

This course is not a museum. It is a tool for the 21st-century musician.

---

## How the course is organized

The course follows the pedagogical sequence of the Shostakovich-Hernández Medrano method:

1. **Preparatory Module** — Notation, clefs, scales, fundamentals
2. **Triads in SATB** — Four-voice chorale writing, root position and inversions
3. *(Coming soon)* Seventh chords, modulation, chromaticism and much more

Each lesson includes:
- Theoretical explanation
- Demonstration videos
- MIDI practical exercise
- Automatic correction by the Virtual Teacher

---

## The Virtual Teacher

The Virtual Teacher is an automatic correction system that analyzes your MIDI exercises and tells you exactly which errors you made and in which measure.

It doesn't just say "wrong" — it tells you *which* rule you violated, *where*, and *how* to correct it. It's like having master Hernández Medrano reviewing your homework.

---

## How to start?

Follow the order of the lessons. Don't jump to advanced content before understanding the fundamentals — in harmony, as in counterpoint, everything builds on what came before.

When you're ready, move to the next lesson. 👇
